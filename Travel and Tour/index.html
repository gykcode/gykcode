cloud 9

Cloud 9 is one of the best surf spots in the world. Located in Siargao Island, Philippines, this is an incredible wave touched by the Pacific Ocean swell and winds.

book now

sugba lagoon

Sugba Lagoon is one of the top places to visit in Siargao that offers an unforgettable experience at one of the bluest lagoons you’ll ever see!

book now

Taktak Falls

TakTak waterfall ended up being a cool little spot to hang for half a day. You could easily come, look and leave all within 20 minutes but if you explore to the top of the falls and do all of the cliff and tree jumps you will make the long journey from General Luna count.

book now

gayum island

Guyam Island is a small island, located about 2 kilometres away from General Luna. It’s part of island hopping tours, together with Daku Island and Naked Island.

book now

daku island

One such activity is island hopping; Daku Island is the biggest in the trio which includes Guyam and Naked Islands. Daku has fine bone-white sand, emerald waters, and swaying coconut trees, all of which make it the perfect tropical destination. It also has a barangay with friendly locals.

book now

magpupungko rock pools

Magpupungko is a natural rock pool, best visited on low tide, that can be found on the east coast of Siargao Island. It’s one of the most popular tourist spots that can’t be missed on your list of places to visit in Siargao.

book now

affordable hotels

Options for accommodations include double rooms if you need a bit of privacy and dormitory rooms if you want a more affordable choice. All rooms have a seating area, air conditioning, terrace, and shared bathroom.

food and drinks

Expect to get some good food, good things, and good vibes at Goodies. They’re best known for their Mediterranean offerings but they are also a one-stop shop for general merchandise for everyday living.
